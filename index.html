About SafeStep

SafeStep is an intelligent women safety platform developed as part of Hackathon 2026 by Fathima Hiba.T (B.Tech CSE). The system integrates real-time GPS tracking, automated SOS triggering, emergency contact management, and smart safety timers to provide enhanced personal security support. This project demonstrates how modern web technologies can be leveraged to empower women through accessible, responsive, and automated digital safety solutions.
